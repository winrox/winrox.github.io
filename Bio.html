So, I guess this is a little about me...

My name is Mariah Winifred Palangpour, but everyone knows me as Winnie. Right now art is just a hobby for me, but I can always aspire to make money off of what I enjoy someday! I just needed a place to display my work in my own space with no hidden user agreements where some company might have some phony claim to my images. So this is my experimental first website!


I graduated in 2011 from the University of Missouri-Columbia. My degree is a BA in Interdisciplinary Studies with emphases in Biology, Psychology, and Art. I am very happy that I got the chance to study under some fantastic people in the art department. My favorites were Matt Ballou , Bill Hawk, and Nathan Boyer. Some of the art you will see on this site will be projects from the classes I took at Mizzou.


Right now I am living in Fayetteville, AR and working on my first piece that will purchased! It's a razorback oil painting for a friend, but a sale is a sale! I also continue to paint small other things for myself, and all will be posted upon completion.


Learning html5 and CSS for this project has been both fun and frustrating! Thanks to Twitter Bootstrap for making it easy for anyone to make a website.
