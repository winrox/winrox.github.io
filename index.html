Winnie
Artist - Web Developer - Awesome Person

I'm Winnie. Sometimes I'm an artist and other times I'm a programmer! Altough programming is a newer addition to routine, I find that I am very much enjoying building websites and javascript applications. Please check out my projects section to learn more.


Also, I'm from Missouri originally but have loved living in Arkansas and Massachusetts along the way. Now I'm loving the people and the weather of Austin, TX where I love to hike and bike in my spare time.

Some of the art you'll see is from my college years from classes and some is just pure imagination! I'm sure you can tell how much I love bright colors. I mostly work in oil paint and chalk pastels. I love how different the two mediums are but also how you can layer with both to create drastic changes from beginning to end of a project.
