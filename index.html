This is the famous Larry. Many a Mizzou student has had the joy of drawing him. I really like this one. It was my first gallery piece! After I graduated in May 2011 this drawing hung in the Mizzou Bingham Gallery from May 16–August 25, 2011 in "The Larry Show". Unfortunately I never got this piece back after the show ended. I asked a friend to pick it up for me and I never saw her again. Katie do you still have this one?

I love being Winnie Son-of-Leopard!
